Visit The Zoo Today!

The mission of Austin Zoo is to assist animals in need through rescue, conservation and education.

At our rescue zoo, children and adults alike can appreciate our Hill Country setting and enjoy an up-close experience with our rescued animals. Austin Zoo is a private, non-profit zoo, with over 300 animals across a 100 different species. Come visit us!
